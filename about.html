Who We Are

Joerees Farms & Consult is a trailblazer in Ghana’s poultry industry, delivering safe, hygienic, and premium-quality poultry products since 2010. With state-of-the-art facilities and a passion for excellence, we serve Accra and beyond.

In addition to supplying dressed chicken, live birds, and fresh eggs, we also offer consultation services for individuals and agribusinesses looking to start or scale their own poultry ventures. Whether you're just getting started or need help optimizing your setup, our team is here to guide you.
